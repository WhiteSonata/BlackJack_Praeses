
Praeses Technical Sample:

Follows this set of rules:
https://bicyclecards.com/how-to-play/blackjack/

Player starts with 20 dollars and bets are currently at 1 dollar unless doubled.

## Installtion
1) Download latest free version of IntelliJ. <br>
2) Should autoinstall the correct jdk, if not it's openjdk-26.0.1.
Hit the play button. <br>
3) Game will be in the output on the bottom left corner. <br>

## Example Game

Welcome to \$1 Blackjack!
Current Total: \$20.00

--- Your Turn --- <br>
Your Hand: 10♣ Jack♠ (Total: 20) <br>
Dealer shows: 2♥ <br>
Do you want to [h]it, [s]tand, [d]ouble down, or s[p]lit? s

--- Dealer's Turn --- <br>
Your Hand: 10♣ Jack♠ (Total: 20) <br>
Dealer's Hand: 2♥ Queen♥ 2♠ 5♠ (Total: 19) <br>

Final Totals -> You: 20 | Dealer: 19 <br>
Player hand wins. <br>
